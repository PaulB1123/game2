"Help the farmer recover all his chickens" is a game about saving the world from the alien invasion. Farmer`s chickens have been kidnaped by the aliens and now are sent back on Earth but undercover are some alien chickens are falling as well. Click only on the farmer`s chickens and you will save his farm and the world.

This project has been created by Paul Balta during the Basic Animation module in Multimedia and Design at KEA ( Copenhagen School of Design and Technology).

Good Luck and Have fun!
